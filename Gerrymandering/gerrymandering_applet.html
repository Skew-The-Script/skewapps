GERRYMANDERING



Determine if your state gerrymandered its Congressional districts, through the power of simulation.

Get started by selecting your state!



    Applet by   Skew The Script (authors: Max Cembalest, Dashiell Young-Saver, and Chris Olsen)
    Simulation powered by   ALARM redist   (by Christopher Kenny & Cory McCartan)
    Background image by Chris Gash.

Methods: the redist simulator divides states into randomly drawn districts of equal population size. Then, it uses prior election data to find the political lean of the simulated districts.
